APJ ABDUL KALAM

1931-2015

"Confidence and Hard work is the best medicine to kill the disease call failure.It will make you successful person."

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam was an Indian scientist known for his role in the development of India’s defense arsenal. He was the country’s president during the years 2002-2007. He was popular among children because he interacted with many students and inspired them to work hard for a bright future. Dr. Abdul Kalam was a person who was not only a great political leader but also a good teacher and a writer. He had many delicate qualities and visionaries.
